RUM HAM!!

In The 7 Secrets of Awakening the Highly Effective Four-Hour Giant, Today (The Gang Writes A Self-Help Book), Frank reveals the ingredients and recipe to cook a rum-ham.

Ingredients

  • 1 10-pound canned ham
  • 1 hunting knife
  • 3 1-litters bottles of rum
  • 1 handgun
  • 1 box handgun ammo
  • 1 bunch of Italian parsley
